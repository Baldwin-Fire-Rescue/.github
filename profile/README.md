## Baldwin Fire Rescue

**Volunteer fire department serving Baldwin Borough, PA since 2025.**

This organization hosts the internal tools and web applications that keep
our department running — from member rosters and apparatus checks to shift
scheduling and fundraising.

---

### What We Build

All repositories here are private, but here's what powers the department:

- **Member & training management** — roster tracking, training compliance, and certification records
- **Apparatus operations** — equipment checks, fleet management, and vendor contracts
- **Shift scheduling** — coverage tracking and shift dashboards
- **Web presence** — WordPress sites, deployment automation, and content management
- **Leadership tools** — KPI dashboards, board portals, and grant applications
